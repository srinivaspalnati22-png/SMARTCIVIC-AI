About SmartCivic

Empowering citizens with AI-powered tools to report, track, and resolve civic issues efficiently

🎯 Our Mission

SmartCivic is revolutionizing civic engagement by combining cutting-edge AI technology with grassroots community action. We believe that every citizen should have a voice in improving their community, and that technology should make it easier—not harder—to report and resolve civic issues. Our platform uses advanced Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision to automatically categorize complaints, route them to the appropriate authorities, and provide real-time tracking for maximum transparency and accountability.

🔄 How It Works

1. Citizen Reports Issue

Citizens can report civic issues through text description, voice input, or by uploading photos. The platform automatically captures location data.

2. AI Analysis & Classification

Our NLP model analyzes the text description, while the CV model processes uploaded images. The system categorizes the issue and assigns appropriate priority.

3. Authority Routing

Based on the category (Road, Water, Electricity, etc.), the complaint is automatically routed to the responsible department or authority.

4. Ward Assignment

Using reverse geocoding, the system identifies the specific ward or administrative area and assigns the complaint accordingly.

5. Status Tracking

Citizens receive real-time updates via SMS/WhatsApp as the complaint moves through stages: Submitted → In Progress → Resolved.

6. Resolution & Feedback

Once resolved, the citizen is notified and can view the resolution details in the tracking system.
